Our Honourable Teacher

Mr. Mohammad Zeeshan Ansari

Mohd Zeeshan Ansari is currently Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Engineering, Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi. He received M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from DTU, New Delhi and B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from UPTU, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. He has more than nine years of teaching experience. His area of research interest is Code Mixing, Information Extraction and Retrieval, Text Mining, Natural Language Processing and Soft Computing Techniques. His field of Specialization is Information Extraction and Retrieval.
